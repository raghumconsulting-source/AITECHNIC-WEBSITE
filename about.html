Our Story

Built from frustration.
Driven by results.

AITECHNIC was founded in Sydney after watching too many small Australian businesses get sold expensive, over-engineered AI "solutions" that collected dust — or worse, locked them into contracts that didn't deliver.

We come from financial services, operations, and technology backgrounds. We understand what it's like to run a business under compliance pressure, with a small team, trying to do more with less. That's exactly who we build for.

Our approach is simple: understand your actual problem first, then find the simplest AI tool that solves it. No jargon. No unnecessary complexity. Just practical results you can measure.

Our process

How we work with you

01

Free Discovery Call

We spend 30 minutes understanding your business, your biggest pain points, and where time is being lost. No pitch, just listening.

02

AI Readiness Assessment

We map your workflows, identify automation opportunities, and assess your existing tools. We tell you honestly what's worth doing.

03

Build & Implement
