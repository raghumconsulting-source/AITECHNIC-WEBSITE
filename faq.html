Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers! Explore our FAQs to find everything you need to know.

General questions

AITECHNIC is an Australian AI consulting and implementation company based in Sydney. We help small and medium businesses identify practical, high-impact uses of AI — and then actually implement them. From automating repetitive admin tasks to setting up AI-powered customer service, we translate AI from buzzword to business result.

We primarily work with Australian small and medium businesses — financial advisers, mortgage brokers, accountants, legal practices, real estate agencies, GP clinics, and trade businesses. If you have repetitive workflows eating your team's time, there's a good chance we can help.

Absolutely — that's exactly who we work with. Most of our clients are business owners, not tech people. We handle all the technical setup and explain everything in plain language. You don't need to know how AI works; you just need to know what problems you want to solve.

If your team is spending significant time on repetitive tasks — chasing clients, data entry, manual reporting, or answering the same questions over and over — you're ready. We offer a free discovery workshop to assess this with you honestly, with no obligation on either side.

Services and features

It's a structured audit of your current workflows, tools, and pain points. We identify where AI can realistically save you time or money, and give you a prioritised roadmap — so you know exactly what to do and in what order, before spending anything on implementation.

Most projects take between 4 and 8 weeks from kickoff to go-live. Simpler automations can be live in as little as 2 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your discovery workshop based on your specific situation — we don't believe in dragging projects out.

Yes. We work with most common business tools — Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Xero, HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, and many others. We assess your existing stack first and build around it wherever possible. We're tool-agnostic; we recommend what actually fits, not what earns us a commission.

Pricing and billing

Every business is different, so we don't believe in one-size-fits-all pricing. We start with a free discovery workshop to understand your specific situation, goals, and challenges — from there, we tailor a proposal that fits your business and budget. Whether you need a quick-win automation or a full AI transformation, we'll scope something that makes sense for you.

Yes — the best first step is our free discovery workshop. It costs nothing, takes around 30 minutes, and gives you an honest picture of where AI can genuinely help your business — and where it can't. There's no obligation and no sales pressure. If there's a good fit, we'll talk through options. If there isn't, we'll tell you that too.

Most clients see measurable returns within 3 to 6 months. Common outcomes include saving 8–15 hours per week on admin, reducing client enquiry response times, and cutting document processing time significantly. We'll always give you a realistic estimate based on your specific workflows before any work begins — we don't promise outcomes we can't back up.

You get full documentation and a handover session so your team can run things confidently. If you want ongoing support — monitoring, optimisation, new automations as your business grows — our retainer covers that. If you just want a one-off project, that's completely fine too.

Privacy and security

This is something we take seriously, especially for clients in financial services, legal, and healthcare. We only recommend tools with appropriate data handling standards, and we configure them to minimise data exposure. We'll walk you through the privacy implications of any tool before it's implemented — no surprises.

Yes — particularly in financial services, where we have deep background. We understand the constraints facing financial advisers, mortgage brokers, and accountants around data handling, client communications, and document management. We design solutions that work within those constraints, not around them.

Never. Your business data is yours. We access only what's necessary to deliver your project, and we operate under a strict confidentiality agreement with every client. We're an Australian-based business subject to Australian privacy law, and we take that seriously.

This depends on the tools we configure for you, and we'll always clarify this upfront. Many enterprise-grade tools offer Australian data residency options. For regulated industries, we specifically recommend tools that meet local compliance requirements and can be configured to keep data within Australia.

Technical support

Minimal disruption is a core part of how we work. We design automations that fit around your existing processes, not the other way around. We also include staff training so your team feels confident — not threatened — by the new tools. Change management is part of every engagement.

Yes. Every implementation includes a handover and training session for your team. We don't just hand you something and disappear — we make sure the people who'll use it daily feel comfortable and capable. Written documentation is always included as well.

Getting started

The easiest first step is to book a free 30-minute discovery workshop. There's no commitment and no cost. We'll listen to what's going on in your business, share our honest view on where AI can help, and give you a clear sense of what working together would look like. Book via the contact page or email us directly at contact.info@aitechnic.com.au.
